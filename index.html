Generative Modeling · Medical Imaging · 2025

Synthetic CT Generation from MRI

Built and iteratively improved a deep learning pipeline for MRI-to-CT synthesis, progressing from a 3D Pix2Pix GAN (3D U-Net generator) to an anatomy-aware SwinGAN (SwinUNETR generator), both paired with a ConvNeXt-based anatomical classifier. Achieved stronger results with SwinGAN across all anatomical regions, with peak performance on the Thorax — outperforming the Pix2Pix baseline with high structural fidelity in complex bone-air interface regions.
